My Photography and Videography Journey

Hong Yih throughoutly enjoys taking photographs and videos, but he believes that photography and videography is so much more than just that. Doodling

An avid doodler, Hong Yih enjoys drawing in his free time, wanting to express himself in the world of drawing and art. He enjoys drawing characters and designing storyboards.
